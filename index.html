Strategic Recommendations

1 Implement Content Format Taxonomy High Impact

Deploy systematic hashtag architecture to track format-level performance and create passive media buying opportunities. Current content lacks organizational structure for brands to identify high-performing segments for future activations. Recommended format categories: #powerslap[event number], #knockoutmoments, #behindthescenes, #athletefeatures.

2 Scale Collaborator Distribution Strategy High Impact

Current athlete collaborator rate: ~6% of total volume (3 collaborations / 50 posts). Industry benchmark for combat sports: 20%. Increasing collaborator posts from 3 to 10 per month would unlock an estimated additional $180K-$240K in monthly EMV through expanded reach. Initial collaborator content performed at account average, indicating scalability without quality degradation.

3 Automate Sponsor Performance Reporting Quick Win

Implement monthly auto-generated sponsor dashboards with direct brand access. This enables performance-based deal structures tied to agreed CPM benchmarks tracked in real-time. Current manual reporting creates lag in optimization cycles and limits upsell conversations. Automated reporting reduces internal lift by 1,750+ hours annually while improving partner transparency.

4 Build Event-Based Content Repository Medium Impact

Create dedicated hashtag system for event nights (#PS14, #PS15, etc.) to build long-term case study repository. This allows content and commercial teams to analyze format performance across event cycles, identify sponsorship integration best practices, and deliver data-backed recommendations to partners. Historical event data becomes sellable asset for prospecting.

5 Deploy Quarterly Projection Framework High Impact

Utilize AVI's projection feature to set quarterly EMV targets and optimal posting cadence. This shifts team from reactive content creation to strategic output aligned with revenue goals. Recommended Q1 2026 target: $1.8M EMV (71% increase) through format optimization and collaborator scaling. Monthly check-ins ensure teams remain on track toward valuation-driving KPIs.

6 Execute Multi-Platform Integration Scale Play

Extend framework to TikTok, X (Twitter), YouTube, and Facebook. Current Instagram-only tracking leaves significant value unattributed. Cross-platform integration provides complete brand exposure visibility and unlocks bundled partnership packages. Implementation sequence: TikTok (Week 1), YouTube (Week 2), X (Week 3), Facebook (Week 4).
